# FIND-FOCUS-FIRE Framework

My systematic approach to AWS vulnerability research.

## FIND 🔍 (Reconnaissance)
Map the AWS service as a system.
- What is its stated purpose?
- What are its parts and interconnections?
- Where does it touch other services?

## FOCUS 🎯 (Analysis)
Find the misalignment.
- Where does actual behavior differ from stated purpose?
- What is the impact of that misalignment?
- Rank by: Misalignment × Impact = Priority

## FIRE 🔥 (Exploitation)
Act with precision on the highest priority target.
- Document everything
- Build repeatable tools around findings
- Report responsibly

---
*Find what others overlook. Focus where it matters. 
Fire with precision.*
